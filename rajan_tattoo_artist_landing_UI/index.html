FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting your tattoo

How much does a tattoo cost?

Tattoo pricing varies based on size, complexity, and placement. Small tattoos start at $150, medium designs at $350, and large pieces/sleeves at $800+. During your free consultation, we'll provide an accurate quote based on your specific design and requirements.

Does getting a tattoo hurt?

Everyone's pain tolerance is different, but most people describe it as manageable discomfort rather than severe pain. The sensation varies depending on placement ‚Äì areas with more muscle and fat are typically less sensitive. We work at a pace that's comfortable for you and take breaks as needed.

How long does a tattoo take to heal?

The outer layer of your tattoo typically heals within 2-3 weeks, but complete healing takes 3-4 months. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and a premium aftercare kit with every tattoo. Following proper aftercare is crucial for the best results and vibrant colors.

Can I bring my own design?

Absolutely! We love working with your ideas and reference images. Whether you have a complete design or just a concept, we'll collaborate with you to create a custom tattoo that matches your vision. We can also refine and adapt designs to work better as tattoos and complement your body's natural contours.

What should I do before my tattoo appointment?

Get a good night's sleep and eat a solid meal before your appointment. Stay hydrated and avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior. Don't take blood-thinning medications (like aspirin) unless prescribed. Wear comfortable clothing that provides easy access to the tattoo area. Moisturize your skin in the days leading up to your session.

Do you offer touch-ups?

Yes! We include one free touch-up session within 6 months for medium designs and within 1 year for large pieces/sleeves. Touch-ups are typically needed only if minor adjustments are required after healing. This ensures your tattoo looks perfect and maintains its quality over time.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. A deposit is required to secure your appointment and will be forfeited if you cancel with less than 48 hours notice or fail to show up. The deposit is applied to your final tattoo cost. We understand emergencies happen ‚Äì please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.
